Look-Alike Modeling

By Michael Teixeira

What is Look-alike Modeling?

Look-alike modeling is the method used in online advertising & marketing for finding audiences who look/act like your most ideal customer. The way it works is through the utilization of data collected through websites and various algorithms. This technique can be broken up into 3 basic steps:

Step 1:

- Collecting as much data as possible. The more data, and the more diverse the data that is available, the more accurate the results provided.

Step 2:

- Define the characteristics of your target audience, your ideal customer. The more specific you get with the characteristics, the higher the chance of successfully attracting the desired audience. It's worth noting however that the more specific you get, the smaller the audience will likely be.

Step 3:

- The last step is to utilize all the info you have gathered thus far, and plug it into your algorithms. Through machine-learning, the algorithms will look to extend/tailor the audience based off of the provided look-alike model.
